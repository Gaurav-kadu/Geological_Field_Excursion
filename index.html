About

This Web GIS platform is created to support geology students and researchers in exploring Rajasthan's rich geological heritage through educational content and visual resources.

Field Excursion Sites

Aravalli Range Precambrian

Fold mountains featuring marble, quartzite, and schist.

  • Region: Udaipur, Alwar

Guru Shikhar Granitic Dome

Highest peak of Aravallis with granitic exposure and structural features.

  • Region: Mount Abu

Nakki Lake Tectonic Lake

Lake formed due to tectonic activity, surrounded by metamorphic rocks.

  • Region: Mount Abu

Rajsamand Morwar Marble Economic Geology

Notable white marble mining area with regional geological significance.

  • Region: Rajsamand

Kabita Formation Stratigraphy

Important stratigraphic unit comprising sandstone, shale, and minor limestone.

  • Region: Nagaur Basin
